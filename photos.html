THE FEBRUARY 16TH WALK AROUND BASLOW

Below, Mike Wilson's photographs show stones carved to celebrate Lord Nelson's battle victories. The rocks were carved by local residents in 1810 to commemorate the Battle of Trafalgar. The three large rocks bear the names 'Victory', 'Defiance' and 'Royal Sovereign" and represent three ships.
